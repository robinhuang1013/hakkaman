Hacker in Taiwan

The hacker introduced this time is a high school student. Although he is a high school student, he has participated in many CTF (Capture the Flag) and server rescue missions.
Why he wants to be a hacker
When he was a child, his father had been instilling knowledge of computers and programming, which made him interested in programming since he was a child.
How he became a hacker
Because his family owned a bakery, he had no money for cram school since he was a child, let alone programming language, but in order to fulfill his dream, he went to the library to borrow books and learn on the Internet.
What has he done
He joined WNM(World Network Maintenance Organization) in April 2020. He participated in many server rescue missions and hacked the vulnerabilities of major community websites and repaired them.
